Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We have answers. Find key details about our admissions, curriculum, and transport systems below.

What are the admission age criteria for each class?

Our age criteria for the academic term are:
- Playgroup: 1.5 to 2.5 Years
- Nursery: 2.5 to 3.5 Years
- Junior KG: 3.5 to 4.5 Years
- Senior KG: 4.5 to 5.5 Years

What is the fee structure for the courses?

We charge a one-time admission fee of ₹5,000. The quarterly tuition fees are: Playgroup (₹18,500), Nursery (₹20,000), Junior KG (₹22,500), and Senior KG (₹25,000). Material & activity charges are billed annually, and optional transport/dining can be opted for monthly.

Are transport facilities safe and GPS-enabled?

Yes. All our school transport shuttles are GPS-tracked and fitted with cameras, speed governors, and seat belts. Each route is accompanied by a certified driver and a female support caretaker who coordinates entries/exits via our parent app.

What are the daily school timings?

Our standard preschool hours are Monday through Friday, 08:30 AM to 01:30 PM. Children in extended daycare programs can stay up to 06:00 PM under supervised guidance.

What safety measures are implemented on campus?

Child security is our first priority. Our gated campus features 24/7 CCTV surveillance in all corridors and play areas, biometric access gates, childproofed furniture with rounded corners, active safety officers, and a pediatrician-approved medical station.

What qualifications do the teachers hold?

All our lead educators hold early childhood care/education (ECCE) certifications, Montessori training credentials, or B.Ed. degrees. They also undergo mandatory background checks, regular pedagogical training, and pediatric first aid certification.

Is extended daycare facility available?

Yes, we offer daycare services from 01:30 PM to 06:00 PM. It includes supervised resting zones, dynamic reading activities, nutritional snack meals, and structured indoor play, suitable for working parents.

What is your general curriculum approach?

We blend traditional Montessori methodologies with experiential, activity-based early learning tools. This approach encourages children to investigate, construct, and articulate logic rather than rely on rote copying.

Playgroup Learning Track

Age Group: 1.5 - 2.5 Years

Learning Focus

A playgroup curriculum focuses on tactile experiences, basic motor development, speaking, and social adaptability. The classroom is free-flowing and filled with safe foam blocks and toys.

Expected Outcomes

  • Develop gross motor skills (crawling, basic balancing, holding small wooden pegs).
  • Improve verbal abilities through sing-alongs, rhymes, and interactive actions.
  • Social adjustments (sharing toys, greeting teachers, sitting in child groups).
  • Basic toilet routines and independent cleanup instructions.

Nursery Learning Track

Learning Focus

Nursery introduces interactive art-based education, pre-writing pencil holding, color/shape recognition, and dramatic role-plays to build emotional independence.

  • Phonics sounds for letters A to Z and simple word identification.
  • Independent painting, clay sculpting, and block coloring skills.

Junior KG Learning Track

Age Group: 3.5 - 4.5 Years

Learning Focus

Junior KG features systematic writing drills, reading basic three-letter words, simple math additions using visual grids, and engaging early environmental science lessons.

Expected Outcomes

  • Basic counting up to 50 and simple additions under 10.
  • Understand local nature concepts (seasons, animals, plant structures).
